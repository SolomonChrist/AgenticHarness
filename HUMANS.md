# HUMANS

Registry of humans who may be involved in tasks, approvals, reviews, or operational follow-up.

Human IDs must use:

- full first name and last name in CamelCase
- followed by a random 4-digit identifier

Example:

- `OperatorExample4821`
- `JaneDoe1044`
