Swarm Training Manual

The Logic Loop

1. You submit a natural language instruction via the Global Composer.
2. MasterBot plans and delegates sub-tasks to specialists.
3. Worker Bots claim and execute work in the background.
4. Results are saved directly into your workspace files/artifacts.

State Vocabulary

NEW: Ready to be claimed by a bot.
IN PROGRESS: Currently being executed.
WAITING: Needs a dependency to finish.
BLOCKED: Failed / needs help.
DONE: Result saved to workspace.
